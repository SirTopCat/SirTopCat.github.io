About Me



I'm Casey Shearer, and I am a junior in high school. I play euphonium for marching band and wind ensemble, and trumpet for fun. I used to play clarinet and sax, but playing with 3 fingers is easier than playing with all 13 of them. I also take Tae Kwon Do.

On a normal weekday, I go to school for 7 hours, go home, and do whatever activity I have that day. Mondays and Thursdays, I head somewhere to eat before marching band rehersal. Sometimes that can be home, or to a food place like Chipotle. Tuesdays I go to Tae Kwon Do, Wednesdays and Fridays are for chilling.

I have a pupper at home named Madison. She happens to be scared of everything, but she's still a happy dog.
