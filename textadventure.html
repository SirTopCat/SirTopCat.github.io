Text Adventure

It is the year 2019 when champion marathon runner, Rabbi Quartz Abraham, must set off on a quest to save her family. Mariel Hassani, a witch and rival competitor, kidnaps and transports R.Q.’s family into Minecraft, a video game set in which the player must build and survive against creatures such as skeletons, zombies, and spiders.

Rabbi Quartz Abraham had beaten Mariel in a race back in last year’s Chicago Marathon and ever since- Mariel has held a grudge against Rabbi Quartz, and due to this year’s loss in the Chicago Marathon, Mariel seeks revenge by keeping her family hostage in a video game. R.Q., as the player, has to kill the Ender Dragon (the final boss) for their release.

Through trials and tribulations, perhaps R.Q. Abraham will stop running away from her problems and face danger, or she will run straight to her demise.
